# Solver_sudoku
In the file sudoku.txt you can write sudoku of any level of complexity in the format:
  8 - - - - - - - -
  - - 3 6 - - - - -
  - 7 - - 9 - 2 - -
  - 5 - - - 7 - - -
  - - - - 4 5 7 - -
  - - - 1 - - - 3 -
  - - 1 - - - - 6 8
  - - 8 - 5 - - 1 -
  - 9 - - - - 4 - -
In unknown cells you must write "-".
